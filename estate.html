REAL ESTATE

About Us

Founded in 1998, Rennford Real Estate has spent more than two decades cultivating a focused presence in frequently difficult-to-access sectors of the market. We target industries supported by enduring secular trends,demographic shifts, evolving consumer behaviors, and structural transformations that fuel long-term growth for real estate investments and services.

Our strategy, however, predates our formal founding. The team had been putting this approach into practice for years before joining Rennford, and we have since invested more than $42 billion in equity across more than 2500 assets spanning a diverse range of industries and property types. This extensive track record provides a foundation of experience that informs every decision we make.



At the heart of our success lies a leadership team with unparalleled depth. Boasting competencies in mergers, capital management, investment analysis, financing, investment sales, investment banking, financial analysis, legal, construction management, and development, our leaders bring an average of nearly three decades of real estate experience to every investment. This combination of sector-specific knowledge and functional breadth enables us to navigate complexity with confidence and execute with precision.

The value-added investment strategy that Rennford pioneered in private equity serves as the foundation for Rennford Real Estate's approach to building enduring value. We have adapted this proven methodology to the unique characteristics of real assets, applying the same discipline, rigor, and operational focus that have defined our firm for decades.



Our team concentrates on small to midsize properties, a niche where our operational expertise can generate outsized impact. In this often-overlooked segment of the market, we deploy deep industry knowledge to accelerate value creation, enhance performance, and unlock revenue potential that others fail to see. Every property receives the same hands-on attention and strategic focus that distinguish our private equity investments.

To further strengthen our insights and sourcing capabilities, Rennford Real Estate draws extensively upon the firm's deep expertise across asset classes and our integrated worldwide platform. Whether leveraging credit team perspectives on financing markets, infrastructure insights on energy and logistics, or private equity operational playbooks, we bring the full weight of Rennford's collective intelligence to every investment.

The foundation of our success, however, remains the extraordinary experience of our senior team. With an average of more than three decades of real estate experience, our leaders possess the judgment that comes only from living through cycles, the networks that form only over decades, and the operational wisdom that distinguishes great investors from good ones. This depth of experience, amplified by the Rennford platform, enables us to navigate complexity with confidence and deliver exceptional results.

This accomplished group of investors brings together a wealth of knowledge, a proven track record, and a shared dedication to thematic, in-depth analytical investing. Their collective experience spanning decades and thousands of transactions enables them to identify opportunities, navigate complexity, and deliver lasting value through disciplined, research-driven decision-making.
