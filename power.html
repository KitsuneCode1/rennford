RENEWABLE POWER

INVESTING IN RENEWABLES TO COMBAT CLIMATE CHANGE

At Rennford, we are actively shifting toward the generation of green power. We recognize that the world needs energy and that how we produce it matters profoundly. That is why we are investing deliberately in renewable energy sources such as solar and wind, generating power that is not only reliable but healthy for the climate. Our commitment to renewable energy reflects our belief that responsible investment can deliver both strong returns and a more sustainable future.

DRIVING RENEWABLE ADOPTION ACROSS OUR PORTFOLIO

At Rennford, we face a reality shared by investors across the global economy: approximately 70% of the businesses we manage currently depend on energy systems that emit harmful gases into the atmosphere. We do not shy away from this fact, we confront it directly, with a clear-eyed focus on solutions.

We are working deliberately to shift our portfolio's reliance on harmful energy solutions toward climate-friendly alternatives. This means investing in energy efficiency upgrades, supporting the adoption of renewable power, exploring emerging clean technologies, and partnering with management teams to implement practical decarbonization strategies tailored to each business's unique circumstances.

Throughout this process, we remain steadfast in our commitment to boosting production and maximizing profits for our investors. We believe that sustainability and profitability are not opposing forces but complementary objectives. By reducing energy costs, improving efficiency, and future-proofing operations against regulatory and market shifts, we create businesses that are not only cleaner but stronger, more resilient, and better positioned for long-term success.

OUR NORTH STAR

By 2040, Rennford aims to decarbonize approximately 95% of the businesses within our portfolio, a fundamental transition toward greener, more sustainable energy that reflects both our environmental responsibility and our long-term strategic vision.

This is not a theoretical exercise. We have already established a dedicated team charged with drafting and executing our decarbonization strategy. These experts work around the clock, engaging with portfolio companies, evaluating renewable energy technologies, and designing practical pathways that reduce emissions while preserving and often enhancing operational performance.

At Rennford, we believe that commitments must be matched by action. That is why we are actively migrating our businesses to renewable energy solutions, investing in the infrastructure and partnerships required to make clean power accessible and reliable for every company we manage.

We are determined to reduce the environmental impact of harmful emissions not only because it is right for the planet, but because it is essential for the communities we serve. By protecting our environment, we protect the places where our employees, partners, and neighbors live and work. This is the purpose that drives our 2040 agenda forward, day after day.
